Spaced repetition is a scientifically-proven learning technique that shows you cards at optimal intervals for long-term retention. Our algorithm tracks your performance on each card and schedules reviews based on how well you know the material:

  • Cards you find difficult appear more frequently
  • Cards you know well appear less often
  • The system adapts to your individual learning pace
  • Review intervals increase as you master content

This approach maximizes retention while minimizing study time.
