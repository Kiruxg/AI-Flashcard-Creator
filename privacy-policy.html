Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Note2Flash ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered flashcard generation service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Data: Any additional information you choose to provide in your profile
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full payment card details)

2.2 Content Data

  • Learning Materials: Text content, documents, images, and other materials you upload for flashcard generation
  • Generated Flashcards: The flashcards created by our AI system based on your content
  • Study Progress: Your learning progress, study statistics, and performance data

2.3 Usage Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP addresses, access times, pages viewed, and actions taken
  • Analytics Data: How you interact with our service to improve functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI flashcard generation service
  • AI Processing: To generate flashcards from your content using our AI algorithms
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process payments, and provide customer support
  • Communication: To send you service-related notifications, updates, and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve our service
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. AI and Machine Learning

Our service uses artificial intelligence to analyze your content and generate flashcards. Please note:

  • Your content may be processed by third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) to generate flashcards
  • We implement measures to protect your data during AI processing
  • We may use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our AI models
  • You retain ownership of your original content and generated flashcards

5.1 Service Providers
